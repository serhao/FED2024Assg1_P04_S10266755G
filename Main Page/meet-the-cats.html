Our Cats!

Fluffy

Fluffy is a scottish fold and is a playful and loving cat who enjoys belly rubs! He was adopted by our cafe in 2022 as one of our employees at that time had found him abandoned near an alleyway. He is turning 3 this year!

Whiskers

Whiskers is a british shorthair who loves to climb and explore new spaces around the cafe. He was given to us by a customer whos cat had given birth to her back in 2021. He is turning 4 this year!

Mittens

Mittens is a Snowshoe who is shy but friendly once she gets to know you. She was found next to Shadow, who is also one of our cats in a box near the roadside and was picked up by one of our Customers in 2019. She is turning 6 this year!

Shadow

Shadow is a bombay cat who is calm and mysterious, often seen lounging in cozy corners. He often only interacts with Mittens, who he was found next to in 2019. Other than that, he often only interacts with certain regulars that he has warmed up to. He is also turning 6 this year!

Leo

Leo is a Devon Rex who loves attention and will always come to greet you when you visit! We adopted him back in 2018 and he is one of our older cats, however, age has not stopped him from having fun and being energetic! He is turning 12 this year!

Simba

Simba is an American Bobtail who is curious and friendly cat who enjoys meeting new people. He was adopted this year and is one of our youngest cats. He is extremely playful and a cafe favourite! He is turning 9 months old this month!
